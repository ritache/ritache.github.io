Hi, I'm Rita!

I'm a UX/UI designer based in Toronto. My passion is to create products that are not only engaging to users, but also to the teams which build, design, and shape the entirety of the experience.

Being a developer in a previous life, I’m very familiar with the expectations and limitations of coding a piece of software. A position in UX gives me the opportunity to blend this knowledge with my love of design and collaborate with the engineering team using the same language (programming or otherwise).

I believe that a cohesive, well-organized UX is vital to the success and evolution of a project. When everyone knows what makes the personas tick, when engineers can find the CSS for that tricky gradient, and designers think putting together a mockup is a breeze with the pattern library, that’s when I know that I’ve done good work as a UX designer.

In my free time I pursue personal illustration projects, play video games, and help my cat down from really high shelves.

If you think I’d be a good fit for your team, or simply want to chat about UX, don’t hesitate to reach out!
